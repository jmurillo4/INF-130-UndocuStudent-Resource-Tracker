Our Mission

Leveling the playing field, one Dreamer at a time.

For immigrants, acclimating to a new country is already a monumental challenge — a new language, new culture, new social codes. For the 510,000 undocumented students currently enrolled in the U.S. education system, that challenge extends into their professional lives too.


Dreamers graduate with their degrees every year, but then face a question most graduates never have to ask: what now? The UndocuStudent Resource Portal exists to answer that question.
